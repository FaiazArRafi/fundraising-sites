Shelter for a family

Cry for relief is getting louder in Sylhet and Sunamganj as at least 40 lakh people remain stranded due to flood in the two districts and over 3 lakh of them have taken refuge at different shelters.

Education for a child

Floods in the north-eastern region are taking a heavy toll on education with at least 5,000 primary and secondary schools in 18 districts closed down.

Children and families need emergency supplies

7 year-old Sihab is one of the 1.6 million children affected by the floods that have devasted north-eastern Bangladesh this year. His entire village was hit with severe flooding following the rainfall, leaving his home underwater. His family is sheltering in a school building, without enough food. “I’ve only eaten dried rice with milk today because there are six of us and not enough rations for all of us,” Sihab says.

Children like Sihab need your urgent support for emergency supplies, nutrition, dry food storage, clean water and hygiene kits. Join us to give hope and emergency relief to children like Sihab.
